# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Reado to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. We pledge to interact in ways that keep
the community open, respectful, and healthy.

## Our standards

We expect everyone taking part — in issues, pull requests, discussions, and any
other project space — to:

- be respectful, considerate, and constructive,
- welcome differing viewpoints and accept feedback gracefully,
- focus on what is best for the project and the community.

Behavior that demeans, harasses, or excludes others is not acceptable.
Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that don't meet these
standards, and may temporarily or permanently ban anyone for behavior they
judge harmful.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces and when an individual is representing the
project in public.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to **matteo@watermelon-studio.it**. All
reports will be reviewed and handled discreetly. We respect the privacy and
safety of anyone who reports an issue.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct adopts the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1. Please refer to it for the full text and enforcement guidelines.
